HoTsPot Water Initiative

Working together with Dutch farmers to protect our water resources

Welcome to Our Water Sustainability Project

Our mission is to preserve and protect water quality in the Netherlands. We focus on reducing nitrogen pollution in groundwater and surface waters by collaborating with farmers across all regions. By using WaterCredits, we aim to foster sustainable farming practices, ensuring a healthier environment and a brighter future for our communities.

Why This Matters

  • High Nitrate Levels: Intensive livestock farming leads to nitrates leaching into water supplies, impacting water quality.
  • WaterCredits System: A practical, market-based way for farmers to offset or reduce their nitrogen pollution—earn credits when you adopt greener methods, or purchase them if you exceed thresholds.
  • Interactive Tools: Our Nitrogen Calculator, Forecast Model, and Water Score reveal how small changes can have a big impact on both your farm’s efficiency and water quality.

Empowering Farmers

We understand the challenges that farmers face daily—financial risk, market pressures, and ever-changing regulations. Our tools are designed to be practical and transparent, showing you exactly how adjustments in feed type, manure management, and grazing practices can reduce nitrogen pollution while helping you save on costs or earn valuable WaterCredits.

By collaborating with farmers, we aim to build a future where responsible water usage and a thriving rural economy go hand-in-hand.

Explore the Tools

Ready to see how simple it is to make a difference? Use our navigation tabs at the top to access:

  1. Pollutant Forecast – View data-driven predictions on nitrate pollution over the coming years.
  2. Farm Game – Experiment with livestock choices and see how your decisions affect emissions and finances in a fun simulation.
  3. Nitrogen Calculator – Enter your farm data and get immediate feedback on your emissions, possible improvements, and potential WaterCredits.
  4. Water Score – Discover how your farming practices rank and identify steps to elevate your score.

Together, we can protect water quality, ensure long-term sustainability, and keep Dutch agriculture flourishing. Thank you for joining us in this effort.
