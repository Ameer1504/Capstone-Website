Water Sustainability Project

Exploring Solutions for the Water Crisis

Welcome! This initiative addresses the urgent need to manage and protect our water resources. Our digital intervention focuses on:

  • Reducing water pollution via WaterCredits
  • Raising awareness of livestock nitrogen emissions
  • Empowering farmers with an interactive calculator
  • Predicting pollutant trends via time-series modeling

Feel free to scroll through this site to learn more about the water crisis and how our Pollutant Forecast Model can guide future sustainability practices.

Key Insights

  1. Nitrate pollution is a pressing issue: The Netherlands, despite advanced water management, faces nitrate leaching into groundwater.
  2. WaterCredits concept: A market-based system that incentivizes farms to adopt sustainable practices or purchase credits.
  3. Interactive Tools: We have a farm game, a Water Score, and a Forecast Model that help visualize the impact of different interventions.
